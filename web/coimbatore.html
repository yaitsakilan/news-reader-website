DIG C Vijayakumar IPS shoots himself dead in Coimbatore

COIMBATORE: Deputy inspector general of police (Coimbatore range) C Vijayakumar shot himself dead with a pistol at his camp office in Race Course here on Friday morning. He was 47.

According to sources, Vijayakumar went for a walk in the morning and came to his camp office around 6.45am. He asked his personal security officer (PSO) to hand over his pistol, and he came out of the office with it. He shot himself dead around 6.50am.

Other cops, who were on duty at the camp office, informed senior police officers who reached the spot.

The body was sent to the Coimbatore Medical College Hospital for postmortem.
